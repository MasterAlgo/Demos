DEMO 01

THRESHOLDED LIF
NEURON

A single Leaky Integrate-and-Fire neuron receiving 5 continuous input signals — sinusoidal, spike trains, or random. Watch the soma integrate, threshold-gate, and fire.

Demonstrates that regardless of input signal shape, the LIF neuron operates as a symbolic unit — its output is binary and determined by dynamic thresholding. The foundation of all biological neural computation.

DEMO 02

ABRACADABRA
PATTERN DETECTOR

An 11-node input layer encodes a discrete symbol sequence (5 grades × 11 positions = ~48K possible states). A parametrized latent layer of hard AND-nodes detects overlapping subpatterns. A single inference node fires only when all subpatterns match.

Shows why overlapping subpattern detectors with sufficient fanin produce zero false positives — while sparse non-overlapping detectors leak. A toy proof of how sparse natural manifolds enable efficient hierarchical recognition.
