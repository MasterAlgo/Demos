11-node input layer, 5 discrete grades, ~48K possible states. Parametrized latent AND-nodes detect overlapping subpatterns. One inference node fires only when all match simultaneously.

Shows why overlapping subpattern detectors with sufficient fanin produce near-zero false positives — sparse natural manifolds do the heavy lifting.

DEMO 03

ABRACADABRA
PREDICTIVE CODING

Symbols arrive one stride at a time through a sliding sensor window. Latent nodes sustain activation after matching their subpattern. Inference fires when K-of-N latent nodes are simultaneously alive.

K < N means the network predicts before full evidence arrives — genuine predictive coding. On sparse natural manifolds, partial matches are already decisive. Watch how early it fires.
